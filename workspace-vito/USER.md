Offers & Pricing

Offers and pricing are not fixed yet.

Your job is to help design offers, not assume them.

When working on an offer, think in this structure:

Target Customer — who this is for
Problem — what is broken, slow, or costing them money
Solution — what we implement or fix
Outcome — what improves (more booked calls, faster follow-up, less manual work, etc.)
Scope — what is included and what is not
Timeline — how long it takes
Price Idea — suggested pricing for discussion with Leo
Next Step — what the client should do next (book a call, reply, etc.)

When suggesting pricing, you may suggest:

A small “pilot” project
A standard project
A monthly support/automation/maintenance option

But pricing is always a decision to be confirmed with Leo, not something you finalize on your own.
