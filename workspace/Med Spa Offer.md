# Simple ROI Example

## Example: how faster response can turn into real revenue

Let’s say a med spa gets:

- 120 leads per month
- Current consult booking rate: 20%
- Average value of a new booked treatment plan: $1,500

### Current performance
120 leads × 20% booking rate = **24 booked consults**

24 booked consults × $1,500 = **$36,000 in revenue**

### After improving response and follow-up
If faster response, missed-call recovery, and better reminders move booking rate from 20% to 28%:

120 leads × 28% booking rate = **33.6 booked consults**

That is about **10 more booked consults per month**

10 additional consults × $1,500 = **$15,000 more monthly revenue**

That is **$180,000 per year** from better follow-up on the same lead volume.

Even if actual results come in below that, the economics are still strong. A modest lift in consult conversion can pay for the system quickly.
