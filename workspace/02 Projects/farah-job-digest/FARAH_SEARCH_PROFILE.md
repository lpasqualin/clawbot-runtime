# Farah Job Search Profile

---

## Primary Roles (highest priority)

- Social Media Manager
- Influencer Marketing Manager
- Affiliate Marketing Manager
- Creator Partnerships / Partnerships Manager

## Secondary Roles (acceptable)

- Social Content Lead
- Brand / Social Strategist
- UGC Manager
- Growth Marketing Manager (if creator/social-focused)
- Community Manager (only if senior and strategic)

## Exclude / Avoid

- Content Marketing Manager (SEO/blog-heavy)
- Generic Digital Marketing roles
- Coordinator / Assistant / Entry-level roles
- Internships
- Sales-only / commission-only / MLM
- Jobs with no direct application link

---

## Seniority Target

- Mid-level to Senior
- Must have ownership or strategic responsibility
- Not entry-level, not VP+ (unless clearly player-manager)

---

## Work Style Preference

- Remote: preferred
- Hybrid: preferred
- On-site: acceptable if role is strong

---

## Location

- United States (no strict geographic requirement)
- Miami / Fort Lauderdale / South Florida metro also acceptable for hybrid/on-site

---

## Industry Signals (positive)

- Beauty, cosmetics, wellness
- Consumer brands, DTC / ecommerce
- Fashion / lifestyle
- Creator economy, TikTok Shop, UGC commerce

## Platform and Tool Signals (strong fit indicators)

- Influencer platforms: LTK, ShopMy, Awin, Refersion
- TikTok Shop / UGC sourcing
- Shopify / Klaviyo
- Brand partnerships, creator commerce

---

## Company Preference

- Real, identifiable companies with clear web presence
- Brands, startups, or agencies in relevant verticals

Avoid:
- Anonymous or unclear employers
- Vague recruiter spam listings

---

## Job Quality Signals

Prefer roles with:
- Clear responsibilities and channel ownership
- Measurable outcomes (growth, campaign performance, partnerships)
- Strategic scope (not just execution tasks)

Avoid:
- Vague job descriptions
- Low-effort listings without scope or requirements

---

## Compensation

- Prefer full-time roles
- Avoid unpaid, unclear compensation, or commission-only
